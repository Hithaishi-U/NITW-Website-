The Department of Electrical Engineering is one of the oldest departments of the National Institute of Technology, Warangal (NITW). Established in 1959 as one of the major departments of the Institute, the department has been actively engaged in teaching and research in diverse fields of Electrical Engineering. With excellent faculty, the department offers Under Graduate (B.Tech) programme in Electrical and Electronics Engineering; Post Graduate (M.Tech) programmes in “Power Electronics & Drives”, “Power Systems Engineering”, “Smart Electric Grid”, and “Control & Automation” (jointly offered with Chemical Engineering Department). The department is actively engaging in research and offers Ph.D. programme and Post-Doctoral programmes in Electrical Engineering.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • M.Tech in Power Electronics and Drives (PED)
  • M.Tech in Power Systems Engineering (PSE)
  • M.Tech in Smart Electric Grid (SEG)
